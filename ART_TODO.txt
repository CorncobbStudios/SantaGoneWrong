Generated after the Level1 rework (real health system, Volcano hazard wired
in, new Yeti enemy, new KrampusBoss). Everything below is currently running
on placeholder/borrowed art so the game is playable - swap files in and
adjust the noted frame ranges in src/utils/Animations.js when real art is
ready. Nothing will break in the meantime.


1. VOLCANO - DONE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Wired up using public/sprites/volcanosheet.png (10x2 grid of 64x64 frames:

takes disc damage and can be killed) rather than a stationary turret.
